About Me

Hello! I'm Mohammad Hossein Khojasteh, and I have recently completed my Master's degree at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST). Currently, I am working as a research assistant at the Data Mining Lab, working under the supervision of Prof. Behrouz Minaei-Bidgoli. My interests lie around Neural-Symbolic models, Explainable AI, Reasoning, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs).
